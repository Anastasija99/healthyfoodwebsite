Cleansing breakfast smoothie

Time to prepare: 4 minutes

Ingredients:

Quantity Ingredient
250ml coconut water
30g vanilla protein powder
15g almonds
1 or 2 kale leaves, stems & spines removed, shredded
1/2 granny smith apple, cored
1 tablespoon lemon juice


How to make The cleansing breakfast smoothie:

  1. Place all of the ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and serve.
